PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 1, 2026

01 // OVERVIEW

This Privacy Policy describes how morantomer.com ("this site," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information. We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your data transparently and responsibly.

This site is operated by Moran Tomer, based in Singapore. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

02 // DATA WE COLLECT

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide through our forms:

  • Mailing list signup: your name and email address
  • Contact form: your name, email address, and message

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools. This site does not collect any data automatically beyond standard server logs maintained by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages), which may include IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps.

03 // HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

The information you provide is used for the following purposes:

  • Mailing list: to send you updates about new book releases, behind-the-scenes content, and other author-related news. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in every email.
  • Contact form: to read and respond to your message.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

04 // THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services to operate this site. Each service may process your data as described:

  • MailerLite (mailing list management) — processes your name and email when you subscribe. MailerLite is GDPR-compliant and maintains a Data Processing Agreement. Their privacy policy.
  • Formspree (contact form processing) — processes your name, email, and message when you submit the contact form. Their privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages (hosting) — serves this website and may log standard access data (IP address, browser info). Their privacy statement.

05 // COOKIES & TRACKING

This site does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or any analytics tools. All fonts and assets are self-hosted — no third-party resources are loaded when you browse the site.

06 // YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: request that we correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request that we delete your personal data
  • Portability: request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: withdraw your consent at any time (for mailing list, use the unsubscribe link in any email)

For EU/EEA residents (GDPR): you have all of the rights listed above. Our legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent, given when you submit a form on this site.

For Singapore residents (PDPA): you have the right to access and correct your personal data. We process data only with your consent.

For California residents (CCPA): you have the right to know what personal data we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal data. We do not sell personal data.

07 // DATA RETENTION

Mailing list data is retained until you unsubscribe or request removal. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and are then deleted.

08 // DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data. All form submissions are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Our third-party service providers maintain their own security measures and certifications.

09 // CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This site is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10 // CONTACT

To exercise any of your rights, ask questions about this policy, or request data deletion, please contact us through the contact form on the main site or email us at the address listed there.

11 // CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
